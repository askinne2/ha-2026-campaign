Campaign Strategy

Phased cultivation · May – December 2026

How Hispanic Alliance is repositioning 2026 fundraising around capacity, timing, and donor experience — not a single compressed launch.

The strategic shift

HA is moving away from a compressed spring launch — June 23 print deadline, July public ask, staff in production mode through Q4 — toward a five-phase cultivation model that separates production, story rollout, private leadership momentum, public launch, and year-end close.

Summer and early fall are for relationship-building and content. The public $10K SDA ask opens September 15. Individual giving concentrates in Q4, where roughly 40% of HA's annual individual revenue typically lands.

Before & after

Previous model

  • June 23 hard print deadline for all mail pieces
  • July 13 public launch + mail drop + email ask
  • Simultaneous SDA + HHM pressure on small staff
  • Production and follow-up competing through December

Revised 2026 model

  • May–June: production only, no public ask
  • July–August: cultivation & story (no public $10K mention)
  • Late August: leadership circle — anchor gifts
  • Sept 15: public SDA launch · Oct 31: donor letter in mail
  • Nov–Dec: follow-up and close (Homes for the Holidays)

SDA: 10 Years of Leaders

Campaign: $10,000 goal ($1,000 per year of the program) · Tagline: "10 Years. Hundreds of Leaders. One Future."

Impact: 339 SDA Fellows statewide · $83,500+ in scholarships awarded · 43 students placed at 19 colleges and universities.

Framing: Future-oriented ("next decade of leaders"), not celebration-only. Landing page: hispanicalliancesc.com/sda

Email cadence (post-launch)

Six story-driven emails, bi-weekly, beginning September 15 — campaign close aligns with HHM handoff mid-September.

HHM 2026 integration

Corporate sponsorships and individual SDA giving are distinct buckets but share a public narrative moment during Hispanic Heritage Month.

  • July 31: Soft close for Presenting & Champion tiers
  • Aug 15: HHM sponsorships largely locked
  • Sept 18: Opening Night Celebration — kickoff for both corporate visibility and community energy
  • Hispanic Líderes Series runs through September

Natural narrative bridge: young leaders (SDA) → Hispanic leaders of today (HHM).

Staff capacity principles

  • Summer/fall = cultivation; Q4 = close
  • By November, staff should be in follow-up mode — not production mode
  • No Giving Tuesday campaign in 2026
  • Homes for the Holidays (December) as individual-giving climax

What we are not doing

  • No public fundraising ask before September
  • No June 23 hard deadline on the task tracker
  • No compressed "everything at once" spring mail drop
