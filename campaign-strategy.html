Campaign Strategy

Five-phase plan · May – December 2026

Hispanic Alliance runs a dual-track campaign: SDA individual giving and HHM corporate sponsorships. Production and cultivation happen in spring and summer; public momentum builds in September; individual giving closes in Q4.

2026 campaign model

The year is organized in five phases from May through December. Summer and early fall focus on story, relationships, and sponsor commitments — not a public fundraising ask. The $10K for 10 Years SDA campaign launches publicly on September 15. Q4 is follow-up and close, where roughly 40% of HA's annual individual giving typically lands.

See the full campaign calendar for dual-track detail (SDA + HHM) by phase.

Five phases at a glance

Phase 1 · May – June — Production & quiet cultivation

Finalize annual report, lock SDA 10-year branding, draft donor letter (no mail yet), build private landing page. HHM: partner calls, prospect list, deck finalized, outreach begins. No donor letter to printer until late August.

Phase 2 · July – August — Soft visibility & story rollout

Annual report email, weekly student/alumni videos on social, landing page live for awareness only. Quiet cultivation with lead donors and board outreach. HHM: active tier outreach; July 31 soft close for top tiers; sponsorships largely locked by August 15. The $10K goal is not mentioned publicly until September.

Phase 3 · Late August — Leadership circle

Small lead-donor gathering to anchor the next decade of SDA before Hispanic Heritage Month. Secure anchor gifts, invite alumni and institutional partners into campaign leadership, prep media and HHM communications. Donor letter to mailboxes October 31.

Phase 4 · Sept 15 – Oct 15 — Public launch

Official SDA campaign launch, six-email story sequence, alumni spotlights peak. Opening Night Celebration (Sept 18) kicks off HHM; Hispanic Líderes Series runs through the month. SDA and HHM are distinct fundraising buckets but share one public narrative moment.

Phase 5 · November – December — Q4 giving season & close

Donor letter already in mailboxes; board cultivation calls in November. Homes for the Holidays as campaign climax. HHM sponsor impact reports and renewal conversations. Staff in follow-up mode, not production mode, by November.

SDA: 10 Years of Leaders

Campaign: $10,000 goal ($1,000 per year of the program)

Tagline: "10 Years. Hundreds of Leaders. One Future."

Impact: 291 SDA Fellows statewide · $83,500+ in scholarships awarded · 43 students placed at 19 colleges and universities

Framing: Future-oriented — the next decade of leaders — not celebration-only

Landing page: hispanicalliancesc.com/sda

Public email sequence (from Sept 15)

Six story-driven emails, bi-weekly, with a specific ask — timed to peak with Opening Night and Hispanic Heritage Month energy.

HHM 2026

Corporate sponsorships and SDA individual giving remain separate fundraising buckets with a shared public narrative during Hispanic Heritage Month.

  • July 31: Soft close for Presenting & Champion sponsorship tiers
  • August 15: HHM sponsorships largely locked
  • September 18: Opening Night Celebration — corporate visibility and community energy for donor cultivation
  • September: Hispanic Líderes Series; GreenvilleJournal.com digital ads live
  • Natural narrative bridge: young leaders (SDA) → Hispanic leaders of today (HHM)

Operating principles

  • Spring and summer = production and cultivation; Q4 = close
  • No public fundraising ask before September 15
  • By November, staff should be in follow-up mode — not production mode
  • No Giving Tuesday campaign in 2026
  • Homes for the Holidays (December) as the individual-giving climax
  • Automated December 31 close email
