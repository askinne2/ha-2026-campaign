"10 Years. Hundreds of Leaders. One Future." · May – December 2026

Hispanic Alliance is running a phased 2026 campaign: production and quiet cultivation in spring and summer, a private leadership circle in late August, public launch aligned with Hispanic Heritage Month in September, and a Q4 close anchored by Homes for the Holidays. SDA individual giving and HHM corporate sponsorships share narrative momentum but remain distinct fundraising buckets.
