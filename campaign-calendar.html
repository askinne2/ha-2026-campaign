2026 Campaign Calendar

Five phases · dual track (SDA individual + HHM corporate)

May through December — production, cultivation, private leadership momentum, public launch, and Q4 close.

HA 2026 revised campaign calendar showing five phases from May through December

Individual giving / SDA Corporate / HHM sponsorships Cultivation & stewardship Events
Phase 1
Production & quiet cultivation
May – June
SDA / individual
  • Finalize annual report copy & stats
  • SDA 10-year logo locked
  • Donor letter drafted (no mail yet)
  • Landing page built — private
  • Jason Ayers video delivery July 1
HHM corporate
  • Existing partner calls — no deck yet
  • Prospect list of 15–20 built
  • Billboard decision resolved
  • External deck finalized
  • New prospect outreach begins
Print vendor deadline slides — no donor letter to printer until late August. Remove June 23 hard deadline from task tracker.
Phase 2
Soft visibility & story rollout
July – August
SDA / individual
  • Annual report emailed (15th anniversary)
  • Weekly student/alumni video drops — social only
  • Landing page goes live — awareness, no hard ask
  • Quiet cultivation meetings with lead donors
  • Board outreach to major supporters
  • Email list built (HA network + donors)
HHM corporate
  • Active prospect outreach — Presenting & Champion tiers first
  • July 31 soft close for top tiers
  • Leaders Series honorees confirmed
  • HHM sponsorships largely locked by Aug 15
No fundraising ask this phase. Content is cultivation. The $10K goal is not mentioned publicly until September.
Phase 3
Leadership circle — private momentum
Late August
Lead donor gathering (breakfast or small event)
  • "Before we launch publicly during Hispanic Heritage Month, we're inviting a small group of leaders to anchor the next decade of SDA."
  • Share campaign vision — future framing, not celebration framing
  • Secure anchor gifts and any SDA sponsorship commitments
  • Invite alumni and institutional partners into campaign leadership roles
  • Prep media + HHM communications
  • Wealth screen HHM Opening Night guest list
This gathering IS the quiet launch. Anchor gifts here fund credibility when the public campaign opens in September. Print vendor deadline moves to here — donor letter to mailboxes Oct 31.
Phase 4
Public launch — HHM + SDA 10-year merge
Sept 15 – Oct 15
SDA public campaign
  • Official $10K for 10 Years campaign launches Sept 15
  • 6-email sequence begins (story-driven, specific ask)
  • Donor letter in mailboxes Oct 31
  • Alumni spotlights + video series peaks
  • Opening Night (Sept 18) as campaign kickoff moment
HHM activation
  • Opening Night Celebration Sept 18
  • Hispanic Líderes Series runs full month
  • GreenvilleJournal.com digital ads live
  • Corporate sponsor recognition activated
  • Post-campaign impact report prep begins
SDA and HHM are distinct fundraising buckets but share the same public narrative moment. Opening Night serves both — corporate visibility for sponsors, community energy for individual donor cultivation.
Phase 5
Q4 giving season & close
Nov – Dec
Individual giving (40% of annual giving happens here)
  • Donor letter already in mailboxes (Oct 31)
  • Board member cultivation calls — Nov focus
  • No Giving Tuesday campaign
  • Homes for the Holidays (Dec) as campaign climax
  • "Next decade" vision messaging — future ask, not celebration
  • Automated Dec 31 close email
Corporate / Summit
  • HHM post-campaign sponsor impact reports
  • Personal thank-you calls — renewal conversation begins
  • Summit date confirmed → outreach opens
  • 2027 planning begins Dec
This is where HA's individual giving actually moves. The summer/fall work is cultivation — Q4 is the close. Staff should be in follow-up mode, not production mode, by November.
